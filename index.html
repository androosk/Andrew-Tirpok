About Me

Andrew Tirpok currently owns Tirpok Cleaners, a chain of retail dry cleaning stores in the Hunterdon County, New Jersey area. Tirpok Cleaners is a family business, started in 1936 by Andrew's grandfather. It's the leading brand in its market. If you live in Hunterdon County, you've probably had something cleaned there or worked there at some point in your life.

As a small business owner for 25 years, Andrew has worn lots of hats. He might go from a marketing meeting in the morning to pressing shirts in the afternoon. He can even fix the shirt press if it breaks down. Mechanical skills, IT Skills, Bookkeeping Skills. The whole package.

When you own a small business, you have to be resourceful.

Andrew is currently seeking new challenges in a different field. It's time for a career change.
